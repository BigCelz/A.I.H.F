ABOUT US

Every community is driven by the hope for the future. However, Alex Ibeh's promising career was sadly cut short by the unpredicted grip of heart illness. His death created a vacuum, but it also gave rise to a determination to alter other people's experiences. Consequently, The Alex Ibeh Heart Foundation was established, serving as both a memorial to Alex and a ray of hope for innumerable hearts.

OUR MISSION

The Alex Ibeh Heart Foundation is dedicated to raising awareness about heart health, providing assistance to those affected by heart illness, and promoting heart disease prevention techniques to reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

OUR VISION

We envision a world in which everyone has the knowledge and skills they need to maintain good heart health, allowing them to live full, active lives free of the shadow of heart disease

ACTIVITIES AND INITIATIVES

The Alex Ibeh Heart Foundation actively participates in a variety of projects to reach its objectives, including educational seminars, community involvement campaigns, and fundraising events. We are committed to spreading knowledge about heart disease prevention, early detection, and treatment options through partnerships with medical specialists, researchers, and local groups.

HOW TO WORK WITH US

There are multiple avenues for individuals and entities to back the mission of the Alex Ibeh Heart Foundation. Offering your time and skills to facilitate events, generate funds, or elevate heart health awareness within your community is invaluable. Contributions play a vital role in underpinning our programs and efforts, empowering us to extend our reach and amplify our impact in combating heart disease. Moreover, we eagerly seek alliances and collaborative ventures with organizations and businesses that mirror our dedication to enhancing heart health and preserving lives.

Become an integral part of our team by volunteering your time, skills, and passion for the cause. Whether you're
organizing events, raising awareness in your community, or providing support to those in need, your dedication as a
volunteer can have a profound impact on the fight against heart disease.

Make a tangible difference in the lives of those affected by heart disease by contributing your financial support. Your generous donations enable us to fund critical research, education programs, and support services for individuals living with heart disease. Every dollar you donate brings us one step closer to a world free from heart disease.

Forge meaningful partnerships with AIHF Foundation to amplify our impact and reach even more people in need. Whether you're a corporation, nonprofit organization, healthcare provider, or individual, there are countless opportunities for collaboration to advance our shared mission of promoting heart health and preventing cardiovascular disease. Together, we can leverage our collective resources and expertise to make a lasting impact on heart health worldwide.
